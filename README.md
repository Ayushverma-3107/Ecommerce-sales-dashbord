# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project is an interactive **Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** developed in **Microsoft Power BI Desktop**. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, customer insights, profit analysis, product category performance, and payment method trends.

The goal of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders make data-driven decisions by visualizing key business metrics in a clear and professional format.

---

## 🚀 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard displays the following business metrics:

| KPI | Value |
|------|--------|
| Total Sales Amount | 32K |
| Total Quantity Sold | 236 |
| Total Profit | 4117 |
| Average Order Value (AOV) | 6255 |

---

### 🔹 Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically analyze data using:

- Quarter Filter (Q1, Q2, Q3, Q4)
- State Filter
- Cross-Filtering Between Visuals
- Drill Down Analysis

---

### 🔹 Visualizations Included

#### 1. Sales by State
Displays total sales amount across different states.

**States Included:**
- Maharashtra
- Madhya Pradesh
- Delhi
- Uttar Pradesh
- West Bengal

**Purpose:**
Identify top-performing geographical regions.

---

#### 2. Quantity by Category
A donut chart showing product category contribution.

**Categories:**
- Clothing
- Electronics
- Furniture

**Purpose:**
Analyze product demand distribution.

---

#### 3. Profit by Month
Monthly profit comparison using column charts.

**Months:**
- January
- February
- March

**Purpose:**
Track profit growth trends over time.

---

#### 4. Sales by Customer
Displays sales generated by individual customers.

**Purpose:**
Identify high-value customers and customer contribution.

---

#### 5. Quantity by Payment Mode
Analyzes order quantities based on payment methods.

**Payment Modes:**
- COD
- UPI
- Debit Card
- EMI
- Credit Card

**Purpose:**
Understand customer payment preferences.

---

#### 6. Profit by Sub-Category
Shows profitability across product sub-categories.

**Sub-Categories:**
- Printers
- Phones
- Accessories
- Bookcases
- Saree

**Purpose:**
Identify most profitable products.

## 📈 Business Insights

### Sales Analysis
- Maharashtra generates the highest sales revenue.
- Clothing contributes the highest quantity sold.

### Customer Analysis
- A small number of customers contribute significantly to revenue.

### Profit Analysis
- Profit remains consistent across the selected months.
- Some sub-categories generate substantially higher profits than others.

### Payment Analysis
- COD is the most preferred payment method.
- UPI and Debit Card transactions are also significant.
